Human Creative Origin.

∴IA certifies the origin of authorship. We provide a voluntary standard to distinguish works created by human ingenuity from those generated by machines.

∴IA / ORIGIN — Standard

Version 1.0 Effective: 2025-12-25 Authority: ∴IA

1. Scope

This standard specifies requirements for certifying that a creative work is of human creative origin. It applies to photography, film, audio, written works, and digital media.

2. Normative Principle

A work conforms to this standard when the primary creative decisions and final expressive content are determined by a human author. Tools may assist execution, but automation must not determine expressive substance.

3. Conformance Levels

  • • HUMAN: No generative AI used for expressive content. All elements directly human-authored.
  • • HUMAN-LED: Human authorship primary. AI used only for analysis or enhancement (noise reduction, etc.).
  • • DISCLOSED: Generative AI used, but fully disclosed. Human direction remains present.

Disclaimer

∴IA is a voluntary certification mark and not a government authority. This standard certifies origin of authorship, not quality or legality.
