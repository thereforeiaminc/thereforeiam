Official Documentation / v1.0

1. Purpose

This standard establishes the criteria for certifying works of Human Creative Origin. It serves to protect the economic and cultural value of human labor.

2. Definitions

Category A

HUMAN

No generative AI used for expressive content. All elements directly authored or captured by human agency.

Category B

HUMAN-LED

AI utilized only for technical enhancement or non-expressive workflows. Primary creative decisions remain human.

Category C

DISCLOSED

Generative AI used as a core component, with clear disclosure provided by the author to the viewer.

Voluntary Compliance

∴IA / ORIGIN is a self-certification protocol. By using the mark, creators attest to the veracity of their work's origin under penalty of reputational loss.
